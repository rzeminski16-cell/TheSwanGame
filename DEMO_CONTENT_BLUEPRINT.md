# DEMO_CONTENT_BLUEPRINT.md
# Demo Scope Definition
# Target Playtime: 45–60 minutes
# Max Player Level: 5

---

# 1. PLAYER BASELINE

## Starting Stats
Health: 100
Stamina: 100
Damage: 10
Attack Speed: 1.0
Move Speed: 120
Crit Chance: 5%
Dodge Chance: 5%

## Weapon System
- 1 auto-fire primary weapon
- 2 active abilities
- Weapons cannot be swapped inside dungeon

---

# 2. ENEMY TYPES (3 TOTAL + 1 BOSS)

## 1. Cave Rat (Melee)
Role: Basic swarm enemy
Low health
Low damage
High spawn count

## 2. Spitter Crab (Ranged)
Role: Zone control
Medium health
Projectile attack

## 3. Cave Brute (Elite Melee)
Role: Tank enemy
High health
Slow
High damage

## Boss: Crab King
Large health pool
Two phases:
- Phase 1: Melee slam + adds spawn
- Phase 2 (50% HP): Projectile spray + faster attack

Boss must feel strong but beatable at level 3–4.

---

# 3. PASSIVE ITEMS (10 TOTAL)

## Damage Focus
1. Rusty Ring (+10% damage)
2. Sharpened Tooth (+5% crit chance)

## Speed Focus
3. Light Boots (+10% move speed)
4. Adrenal Charm (+10% attack speed)

## Defense Focus
5. Thick Jacket (+15 health)
6. Lucky Coin (+5% dodge)

## Hybrid
7. Balanced Blade (+5% damage, +5% attack speed)
8. Hunter Charm (+5% crit, +5% move speed)

## Rare
9. Blood Pendant (+15% damage, -5% dodge)
10. Golden Idol (+10% all stats)

No item may exceed 25% bonus in demo.

---

# 4. SKILL TREE (15 NODES TOTAL)

## Combat (5)
- +5% Damage
- +5% Attack Speed
- +5% Crit Chance
- +10 Health
- +5% Move Speed

## Economy (5)
- +10% Delivery Reward
- -10% Rent
- +5% Loot Chance
- +10% Money Drop
- +5% XP Gain

## Personality (5)
- +5% Dodge
- +10 Stamina
- +5% Boss Damage
- +5% Elite Damage
- +5% Movement in Dungeon

Each node = 1 level only.

---

# 5. DUNGEONS

## 1. Crab Cave (Story Dungeon)
Type: Story
Replayable: No
Completion Count: 1

Rooms:
- 2 combat rooms
- 1 elite room
- 1 boss room

Unlocks replayable dungeon after completion.

---

## 2. Abandoned Tunnel (Replayable)
Type: Replayable
Scaling:
- Increases per completion
- Max difficulty level in demo: 5

Structure:
- 3–5 combat rooms
- Randomized enemy groups
- No boss

Reward:
- Better loot each completion
- Higher XP

---

# 6. DELIVERY MINI-GAME

- 3 delivery points
- Static spawn locations
- No time limit
- Base reward: 50
- Reward scales with economy skills

No police system in demo.

---

# 7. STORY MISSIONS (5 TOTAL)

1. Move In (Tutorial)
2. Find Papers
3. First Delivery
4. Enter Crab Cave
5. Pay Rent

Story progression tied to dungeon completion.

---

# 8. ECONOMIC PRESSURE

Weekly Rent:
250 base

Player must:
- Deliver
- Dungeon
- Loot sell
- Complete missions

Goal:
Create light pressure but not failure spiral.
